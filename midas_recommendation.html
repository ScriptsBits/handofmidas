Significance
Observational data only — not a randomised trial. A negative delta means players who bought Midas in this situation won less often than non-Midas players in similar positions. It does not prove Midas caused the loss. Selection bias is present: players who commit to Midas when losing may differ systematically from those who adapt and skip it. Use as a signal, not a verdict.   Role model mode uses logistic regression to smooth sparse cells (features: buy minute, personal NW adv, team NW %). Pseudo‑R² values are in the model panel.
